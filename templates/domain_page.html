LIFE PERCEIVES

Life Perceives is a symposium that brings together scientists and artists to explore the concept of perception across all forms of life.

Through short talks, discussions, and artistic interventions, attendees will gain unique insights into the life worlds of plants and fungi, microbial communities, and immune systems.

An accompanying photography exhibition by Irina Petrova Adamatzky and an installation by Sissel Marie Tonn will also be featured.

THE BRAIN PRIZE SERIES

We are excited to announce that WWN will continue to feature The Brain Prize series in its 2nd term / iteration, running through 2022-2023.

The series will focus on this year's winners of the prestigious prize, and their revolutionizing contributions to our understanding of movement / motor control.

The Lundbeck Foundation is a major sponsor of our WWN endeavour and we are grateful for their continued support and commitment to bring top-notch scientists to our virtual auditoria.
